## Performance

- **Document search is now faster and more accurate.** Search index entries are built per page as documents finish processing, so results are available sooner and more precisely scoped.

## AI & Automations

- **Name matching is more accurate.** Canonical name resolution has been improved, reducing mismatches when automations try to identify entities across documents.
- **Automations are more resilient.** Concurrency limits are now enforced more carefully, and filter shapes from earlier automation steps are correctly preserved when passed downstream — reducing silent failures in multi-step workflows.
- **Match quality improved for structured data.** Trigram, numeric, and date postings are now precomputed per page, making structured data lookups in automations faster and less error-prone.

## Bug Fixes

- **Malformed filter values no longer cause automation crashes.** Invalid inputs are now rejected with a clear error instead of taking down the query.
- **Fewer document processing failures.** Hardened handling of non-standard text encodings and oversized token batches means documents with unusual characters or large text blocks are less likely to fail during AI processing.
